In-class Activity

Working in pairs, come up with XPath expressions to find answers to the following questions about a complex XML document

  1. What's this finding aid's unique identifier (Beginning with InU-Ar...)? /ead/eadheader/eadid
  2. Who prepared this finding aid ("Finding aid prepared by...")? /ead/eadheader/filedesc/titlestmt/author
  3. What's the full version of the corporate name of the repository (the one with the dates at the end)? /ead/archdesc/did/repository/corpname/@normal
  4. How many boxes are in this finding aid according to the physical description? /ead/archdesc/did/physdesc/extent[2]
  5. How many boxes are in this finding aid according to the container list? count(//container[@label="Box"])
  6. How many items are in this collection? count(//c02)
