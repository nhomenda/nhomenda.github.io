A simple XML document.

In-class Activity

Working in pairs, come up with XPath expressions to find answers to the following questions about a complex XML document

  1. What's this finding aid's unique identifier (Beginning with InU-Ar...)?
  2. Who encoded this finding aid ("Finding aid prepared by...")?
  3. What's the full version of the corporate name of the repository (the one with the dates at the end)?
  4. How many boxes are in this finding aid according to the physical description?
  5. How many boxes are in this finding aid according to the container list?
  6. How many items are in this collection?
