Proposal (250-500 words)

Write a short proposal outlining your project. Your proposal should briefly discuss:

  • content
  • intended audience
  • significance of project
  • preliminary technological plan for the project

Infrastructure diagram and interface wireframes

Create one or more diagrams illustrating the design and workflow of the technical solution ot your final project.

Create a set of wireframe documents illustrating your initial interface ideas for your project. Wireframes may be hand drawn (though they should still look professional) or produced with software. There are many free and non-free wireframing apps for many platforms.

Deliverables

Please submit your proposal and infrastructure diagrams/wireframes as Word or PDF documents on Canvas.
